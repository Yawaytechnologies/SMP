Company Overview

SMB has been a pioneering provider of smart electrical solutions since its inception. We specialize in smart meter manufacturing, substation erection, and skilled manpower management for industrial, commercial, and residential projects.

Founded in April 2023, SMB Smart Electrical Solutions was built on a mission to combine innovation, operational excellence, and strict compliance to deliver reliable, safe, and efficient energy solutions. From planning to execution, we ensure every project meets the highest standards of quality and client satisfaction.
